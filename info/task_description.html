You are massively sending thousands and thousands emails every day, meanwhile experimenting with subject lines and the message itself. SendGrid allows you to see statistics in your spam reports.

Your mission is to figure out how many spam reports you receive on a specific day.

Input: Day as a string in format 'YYYY-MM-DD'

Output: Int. The amount of days

Example: 

    how_spammed('2014-5-5') == 16
